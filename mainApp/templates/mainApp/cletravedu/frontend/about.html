Cletrav Education Partnership Program Overview

Cletrav Education Co., Ltd. is excited to extend an invitation to institutions, universities, tech companies, and educational organizations worldwide to join forces in our International Student Mobility Programs (ISMPs) and International Software Engineering Program. Together, we can empower students with the skills, global exposure, and real-world experience needed to thrive in today's competitive digital economy.

Our Partnership Program is designed to create mutually beneficial collaborations that enrich the educational journey of young talents and open up diverse opportunities for partner organizations. Here's how your institution or organization can benefit from partnering with us.

Why Partner with Cletrav Education?
  • Global Talent Development:
  • Contribute to shaping the next generation of tech professionals equipped with global knowledge, practical skills, and cross-cultural competence.
  • Your organization can play an integral role in training and developing skilled graduates who are well-prepared for international markets.

  • Access to High-Potential Students:
  • Our students go through a rigorous, hands-on, and industry-focused curriculum. By partnering with us, your organization gains access to a pool of well-prepared, motivated, and talented individuals for internships, training, and employment opportunities.

  • Shared Brand Value:
  • Aligning with Cletrav Education positions your institution or organization as an advocate for innovation, education, and international collaboration, which can elevate your brand in the educational and tech communities.

Program Structure and Benefits for Partners
  1. Academic Partnerships with Universities and Institutions:
    • Objective: Enable seamless academic transition for students studying in Nigeria, and Vietnam.
    • Role: Host students during their degree progression, allowing them to complete semesters and gain academic credits towards their B.Sc. in Information Technology or Data Science.
    • Benefits: Gain enrollment from international students, foster cultural diversity on campus, and expand your institution's global reach.

  2. Industry Partnerships with Tech Companies and Organizations:
    • Objective: Offer hands-on internships, mentorship, and job placements to students in our programs, providing them with real-world experience.
    • Role: Host our students for internships or job placements, participate in curriculum input to ensure skill relevance, and offer guest lectures or workshops.
    • Benefits: Gain access to a pipeline of tech-ready talent, enhance corporate social responsibility (CSR) efforts, and influence the practical skills taught to future tech professionals.

  3. Internship and Employment Partnerships:
    • Objective: Provide students with a bridge to employment by offering work placements that are an essential part of their academic and practical training.
    • Role: Create internship programs that integrate with our curriculum, helping students apply their knowledge in real-world contexts.
    • Benefits: Tap into a diverse group of students ready for entry-level roles, shape the talent pipeline to meet your industry needs, and build a direct pathway for recruiting skilled graduates.

  4. Technology and Resource Partnerships:
    • Objective: Support our programs with access to tech tools, educational resources, and industry insights that enrich our students' learning experience.
    • Role: Provide access to software tools, platforms, digital resources, or funding for educational initiatives.
    • Benefits: Promote your products and tools within an educational setting, and support tech education through active engagement with our students and staff.

Cletrav's Commitments to Partners
  1. Tailored Collaboration Plans:
    • We design partnership structures that align with your organization's goals, ensuring that your involvement with Cletrav Education is impactful and mutually beneficial.

Becoming a Cletrav Education partner allows your organization to make a lasting impact on international education and tech talent development. Together, we can create a world where education is accessible, relevant, and transformative.

About Cletrav Education

Cletrav Education Co., is a pioneering international education technology company focused on equipping young talents with industry-ready tech skills through our International Students Mobility Programs (ISMPs). Headquartered in Abuja FCT, Nigeria, we provide a unique pathway for students to start their further education locally and complete their degree abroad, earning a B.Sc. in either Information Technology or Data Science.

Our programs emphasize hands-on learning, small class sizes, and real-world industry experience, empowering graduates with global skills and career opportunities in the digital economy. At Cletrav, we're dedicated to transforming education and bridging gaps in global tech talent.
