Foundation semester study in Nigeria

Start, study 01 semester year 1 in Nigeria.This is an intensive hands-on-practical learning experience building you on all industry best-practices in software development. You will cultivate working skills to meet the practical demands of the industries in our host nations.

Continue 2nd Semester in Vietnam

Transfer to international partner universities in Vietnam to continue from 2nd semester year 1 to year 2, spending about 18 months. At this stage, students will enhance on the skills acquired from the 1st semester while in Nigeria, through a 3-months internship. Successfully completing the internship gets you working and earning while still studying.

Final year in America

Learn in-depth knowledge according to the original programs of international university partners. On-the-job training with companies. Accumulate working experience within a bigger economic environment. Finish and Receive a prestigious Bachelor's degree in Information Technology upon graduation.

Final year in Australia

Learn in-depth knowledge according to the original programs of international university partners. On-the-job training with companies. Accumulate working experience within a bigger economic environment. Finish and Receive a prestigious Bachelor's degree in Data Science upon graduation.

Frequently Asked Questions

We hope these FAQs provide potential students with a clear understanding of the benefits, logistics, and application process for Cletrav ISMPs.

What is Cletrav's International Student Mobility Program (ISMP)?

Cletrav's ISMP is an educational pathway that allows students to study across multiple countries. The program combines academic learning with practical experience, starting with foundational studies in students' home countries and progressing to advanced coursework and internships abroad. The ultimate goal is to earn a globally recognized degree while gaining valuable international experience.

Who is eligible to join Cletrav ISMPs?

Cletrav ISMPs are open to high school graduates and early-career professionals interested in advancing their skills and gaining an international qualification. Each program may have specific requirements, so please refer to the program details or contact our admissions team.

What countries are included in Cletrav ISMPs?

Cletrav ISMPs offer a multi-country study pathway that starts in the student's home country with practical coursework, progresses to an academic + internships semester in Vietnam, and concludes with final year studies in either the United States, Australia, Canada, England, or the Switzerland. This pathway ensures students gain diverse cultural and professional experiences.

What degree or certification will I receive upon completion?

Upon successful completion of the program, students will receive either a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology from a U.S.-based partner university or a Bachelor's degree in Data Science from an Australia.-based partner university, with recognition in multiple countries. Additional certifications or credentials may be awarded based on the specific course modules completed during the program.

How does Cletrav ISMP support hands-on learning?

Cletrav ISMP emphasizes hands-on, practical learning through internships, project-based coursework, and access to industry-standard tools and technologies. From the first semester, students apply their skills in real-world settings, preparing them for competitive global job markets.

How much does the program cost, and are there financial aid options?

The cost varies by program and country. Cletrav offers flexible payment plans, facilitates access to internship opportunities to reduce the financial burden on students. Contact our admissions team for specific program costs and details on available financial aid.

Will I receive help with visas and immigration?

Yes, Cletrav provides visa assistance and guidance throughout the program. Our team supports students with visa applications, residency options, and immigration paperwork to facilitate a smooth transition between countries.

Can I stay in the U.S. after completing the program?

Yes, many students have the option to pursue residency or work permits upon program completion, depending on individual eligibility and local regulations. Cletrav offers guidance on post-graduation options to help students explore career opportunities in the U.S.

What if I decide not to continue to the next stage of the program?

Students can choose to exit the program at certain stages and still receive credits for completed coursework. However, we recommend consulting with a Course Manager to understand how this may impact career progression or degree completion.

How do I apply to Cletrav ISMPs?

You can apply through our website by filling out an online application form. After submitting your application, you'll be contacted by our admissions team for an interview to discuss your educational background, career goals, and program suitability.

Who should I contact for more information?

For more information, please reach out to our admissions or support team via email or phone, as listed on our website. We're here to answer any questions you may have and help you understand how Cletrav ISMPs can support your educational and career aspirations.
